Who We Are

Built by students,
driven by passion

Velocity Drive is a student-created informational platform developed as a web design capstone project. We are Momentum — a five-member team united by curiosity, creativity, and a deep love for automotive excellence.

Our mission: present the world's most iconic supercars in a clean, immersive, and informative digital experience. Engineering specs, design philosophy, and raw performance — all in one place.
